Landmark

Memorial Library

Beaux-arts reading room housing over 2 million volumes, rare manuscript collections, and 24-hour study spaces.

Facilities

Innovation Lab

State-of-the-art maker space with 3D printers, laser cutters, robotics stations, and collaborative project studios.

Student Life

Student Union

The heart of campus life — dining hall, coffee bar, student organization offices, game lounge, and event spaces.

Academics

Science Center

Modern labs for biology, chemistry, and physics research, plus a rooftop observatory with a 16-inch telescope.

Arts

Performing Arts Center

A 1,200-seat concert hall, black box theater, art galleries, and practice studios for music and dance.

Residential

Residence Quad

Four historic residence halls arranged around a central green with gardens, hammocks, and outdoor gathering spaces.
